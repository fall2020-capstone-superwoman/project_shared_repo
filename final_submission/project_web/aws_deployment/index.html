ABOUT

In 2019, there are nearly 4 million women in the U.S. starting their journey of two-men-diet. According to one research, around 10% of the moms experienced nutrition deficiency during their pregnancy even after taking daily prenatal supplements. Nutrition deficiency are risky to both the mom and the baby, and adding more supplements also have side effects. So better planning each meal is the first option to solve the problem.

OUR MISSION
Make just-in-time nutrition choices easy for new moms.

OUR PRODUCT(MVP)
An on-demand and informative nutrition recipe recommendations mobile application.

  • Target-Focused Needs: App focuses on needs of pregnant/lactating women
  • Customization: Allows customization based on individual needs and preferences
  • Minimize User Fatigue: Only two input pages and could be saved for next use

Model Overview and Flow

Unsupervised Model - Recipe Recommendation:
We used the algorithm shingle(n-grams) similarity algo which converts strings into sets of n-grams then the distance are calculated between them. In our case, user ingredients list and recipe ingredients list are converted into two pre-calculated profiles and the cosine similarity between them are calculated. After overlaying with user preference and constraints, the result is ranked by nutrition and show the top three.

Unsupervised Model - Co-occurrence Ingredients Recommendation:
By using gensim model from word2vec, we got top 50 most similar ingredients for each input value. We apply hard constraints, like allergen, exclusion ingredient list, nutrition intake reference and user nutrition ranking preference, then got the final results of co-occurring ingredients in each nutrient category. And user will have a list for ingredients recommendation in the lens of nutrition.

Unsupervised Model - Ingredients Recommendation Model Evaluation
Data visualization of ingredients clusters are generated to evaluate the similar ingredients from the clusters. For example: Mozzarella, Provolone and Pepperoni are in a cluster. From this data viz, we can confirm the clusters are valid.
